Welcome to Prawnsgame.org.uk!

We spent far too much time offline, and are now back with a new, improved, easily updateable site design. Yay. ;)

Over the next few days (weeks?) we will be adding lots of content - getting back to our previous state of a full documentation of the game 'Prawns', and also adding some extra games we've made, such as the newly renamed Kalamala.

We (the people behind this website) are a rough disorganisation of people who, amongst other things, like, or have liked, to design abstact strategy board games and then publish them here. Find out less about us at our About page.

Excitingly, we have started on a project to bring you a digital Kalamara experience. Our Play page will be updated with details as we make progress.
