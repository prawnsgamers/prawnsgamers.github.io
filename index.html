30m up an electricity pylon, the wind whistled around the iron bars as the wires sparked with blue electricity. A large bird tried straddling the gap between the two wires, and instantly turned into a char-grilled chicken, leaving a slightly burned smell in the air. Just there was a large wookie who goes by the name Chewbacca, and standing 3 feet in front of him stood a woman in metre tall high heel shoes known only as THE REALLY TALL PERSON WHO IS ACTUALLY REALLY SHORT, HER SHOES ARE JUST MASSIVE, or TRTPWIARSHSAJM for short. "Oh my good-ness, you look just like a tree I met this morning!" he said to her. Then, having put on his glasses, he said, "Actually, you don't look quite as good."
"17 rubber ducks." Said she with finality. They were about to engage in a world record attempt for fastest time to eat 100 skittles with chopsticks where one person holds each stick, when suddenly, a butterfly flapped its wings on the other side of the world. Expecting a tornado, he threw himself to the floo and clasped his hands over his head. No tornado came. Without wishing to devolve to the now overused "thinking that this was a bit to anticlimactic for a sentance that follows a 'when suddenly'" paradigm, she threw herself into a pit to venomous baby rat snakes. She panitced. The bomb inside her detonated and she was painted over the walls. And so, the newly weds climbed their elephant taxi and proceed to eat it alive as they rode into the sunset.
