Welcome to Prawnsgame.org.uk!

We spent far too much time offline, and are now back with a new, improved, easily updateable site design. Yay. ☺

Over the last few weeks we have been adding lots of content - working tirelessly to get back to our previous state of a full documentation of the game Prawns, and also adding some extra games we've made, such as the newly renamed Kalamala.

We (the people behind this website) are a rough disorganisation of people who, amongst other things, like, or have liked, to design abstract strategy board games and then publish them here. Find out more about us at our About page.

Excitingly, we have started on a project to bring you a digital Kalamara experience. Our Play page will be updated with details as we make progress.
